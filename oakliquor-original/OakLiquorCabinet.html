Welcome to Oak Liquor Cabinet

Oak Liquor Cabinet is located conveniently in NorthWest Austin, in the Jollyville neighborhood between Spicewood Springs and Oak Knoll.

Stop by and browse our large selection of spirits, wines, ale, beer, cigars, cigarettes and lottery tickets. We also carry specialty items like kegs, wine glasses, flasks, and gift baskets.
